Lisa

Born Pranpriya Manobal on March 27, 1997,[3] in Buriram Province, Thailand,[4] she later legally changed her name to Lalisa, meaning "the one being praised",[5] at the advice of a fortune teller for prosperity.[6][7] An only child, she was raised by her Thai mother, Chitthip Brüschweiler, and Swiss stepfather and chef Marco Brüschweiler.[8] Lisa completed secondary education at Praphamontree School I and II.[9] She is multilingual; along with her native Thai, she speaks fluent Korean and English, as well as basic Japanese and Chinese.[10]
